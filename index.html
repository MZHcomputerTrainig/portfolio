I am the founder of the M.Z.H Computer Training Online Class.
Computer training online classes offer individuals the opportunity to acquire or enhance their computer skills and knowledge remotely, using the internet as a platform for learning. Online computer training classes cover a wide range of topics, catering to different skill levels and interests. These courses may include basic computer literacy, software applications (e.g., Microsoft Office suite, graphic design tools), programming languages (e.g., Python, JavaScript), web development, data analysis, cybersecurity, and more.
